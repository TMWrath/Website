Welcome To My Own Little World

Hello! My name is Tyler Melton, and as an artist, I go by the name T.M. Wrath. I'm a multidisciplinary creator specializing in 3D modeling, artwork, and songwriting.

This website is a showcase of my creative journey, where I blend technology and art to bring ideas to life. Here's what you'll find:

  • 3D Modeling: I create detailed 3D models for various applications. For many of my designs, I provide print-ready files so you can bring them to life using your own 3D printer.
  • Artwork: I create original 2D artwork, exploring a variety of styles and mediums to express my creativity and tell visual stories.
  • Songwriting: I write lyrics that capture emotions and tell stories. I also provide proof of concepts for my lyrics, demonstrating how they can be brought to life through music.

Feel free to explore my work, and don't hesitate to reach out if you'd like to collaborate or have any questions. Thank you for visiting!
